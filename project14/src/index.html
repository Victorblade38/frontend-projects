Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the frequently asked questions

What is roadmap.sh?

roadmap.sh is a community effort to create learning paths, guides, project ideas, and other similar content to help developers grow in their careers.

What are the benefits of using roadmap.sh?

It helps developers follow structured paths, ensuring they cover essential skills and concepts required in their careers.

Is roadmap.sh free?

Yes, it is free and open-source, with contributions from the developer community.
